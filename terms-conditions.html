Terms & Conditions

For Tenants of Prakash Apartments

By renting and occupying an apartment at Prakash Apartments, the tenant agrees to comply with the following terms and conditions. These rules are designed to ensure a safe, comfortable, and harmonious living environment for all residents.

1 Residential Use Only

  • Apartments are strictly for residential purposes.
  • Running any business, commercial activity, office, warehouse, or other unauthorized operation from the premises is prohibited.

2 Rent Payment

  • Monthly rent must be paid on or before the last day of the current month for the upcoming month.
  • A grace period of 5 days from the beginning of the new month is allowed.
  • If rent remains unpaid after the grace period, a late fee of ₹100 per day will be charged until the outstanding amount is cleared.
  • Continued non-payment may result in the initiation of tenancy termination proceedings in accordance with the rental agreement and applicable law.

3 Peaceful Living

  • Tenants must maintain a peaceful and respectful environment.
  • Loud music, excessive noise, abusive language, harassment, fighting, or any activity that disturbs other residents is strictly prohibited.

4 Illegal Activities

  • Any illegal activity within the apartment or apartment premises is strictly prohibited.
  • Such activities may result in immediate reporting to the appropriate authorities and legal action.

5 Smoking, Alcohol & Drugs

  • Smoking is not permitted in common areas of the property.
  • Consumption or possession of illegal drugs is strictly prohibited anywhere on the premises.
  • Alcohol consumption must not create disturbance or nuisance to other residents.

6 Cleanliness & Property Care

  • Tenants must keep their apartment clean and hygienic.
  • Damage to the apartment, furniture (if provided), fixtures, or common property beyond normal wear and tear shall be repaired or compensated by the tenant.

7 Visitors

  • Visitors are welcome provided they do not disturb other residents or violate apartment rules.
  • The apartment shall not be used for unauthorized gatherings or parties that create inconvenience to others.

8 Safety

  • Tenants must follow all safety guidelines issued by the apartment management.
  • Any hazardous or flammable materials must not be stored inside the apartment.

9 Common Areas

  • Corridors, staircases, parking areas, rooftops, and other common spaces must be kept clean and free from obstruction.
  • Personal belongings should not be left in common areas.

10 Respect for Property

  • Any intentional damage to the apartment building, equipment, utilities, or common facilities may invite legal action and recovery of repair costs.

11 Notice for Vacating

  • If the management decides to revoke or terminate a tenancy, Prakash Apartments Office will ordinarily provide a minimum of 30 days' prior written notice, except where a shorter notice is permitted under the rental agreement or applicable law due to serious violations.

12 Serious Misconduct

  • Fighting, threatening residents, creating repeated disturbances, damaging property, or engaging in illegal activities may result in immediate disciplinary action, legal proceedings, and termination of tenancy as permitted by law.

13 Compliance with Rules

  • Tenants must comply with all apartment rules communicated by the management from time to time for the safety, cleanliness, and comfort of all residents.

14 Amendments

  • Prakash Apartments reserves the right to update these Terms & Conditions whenever necessary. Any revised rules will be communicated to tenants and shall apply prospectively.
